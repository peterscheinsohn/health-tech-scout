BfArM-based DiGA scout

A practical market map, not a ranking.

DiGA profiles are oriented around the official BfArM registry. Adjacent healthtech companies are kept separate, so the site can show both the regulated DiGA market and the wider digital health context.

DiGA entries are based on the official BfArM registry. Adjacent entries are included only when they help map the surrounding digital health market.

Evidence pilot 01

Hospital Discharge Intelligence

A Power BI and Python pilot using de-identified New York hospital discharge records to explore cost, length of stay, payer mix, diagnosis groups, and provider-level variation.

The pilot shows how public healthcare datasets can surface operational burden and point toward relevant solution areas such as care coordination, hospital analytics, discharge planning, and cost transparency.

Signals explored
  • Median costs and charges by diagnosis group
  • Length of stay patterns across clinical areas
  • Payer and severity differences
  • Provider-level heterogeneity
